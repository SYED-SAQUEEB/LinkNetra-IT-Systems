About LinkNetra
IT Systems

In today’s connected world, reliable networking is the backbone of every modern enterprise — and LinkNetra IT Systems stands at the forefront of delivering secure, intelligent, and future-ready IT solutions across Maharashtra.

We design and deploy end-to-end active and passive networking solutions, including structured cabling, fiber optics, Wi-Fi controllers, enterprise switches, routers, and firewalls — ensuring seamless communication across data, voice, and video with maximum uptime.

Our expertise covers enterprise-grade switches, secure gateways, high-performance copper/fiber cabling, access points, racks, and cable management systems built for long-term stability and scalability.

Businesses choose LinkNetra for transparent service, fast support, genuine software licensing, cost-effective AMC plans, and solutions aligned with global standards.
